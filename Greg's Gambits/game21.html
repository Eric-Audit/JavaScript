In this game, each player begins with a score of 0. The computer will generate two random numbers between 2 and 11 for the player (the player’s cards) and two for the computer (the dealer’s cards). An ace has the value of 11; therefore there is no 1. Face cards (Jack, Queen, King) all have the value of 10.
