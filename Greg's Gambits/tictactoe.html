Tic Tac Toe

This Game is for two players. One player is yellow (X), the other is orange(O). The goal is to get three boxes in a row(across, down, or diagonally). When you enter your letter into a box and click OK, the box will change to your color. The game ends either when one player wins or there is a tie.
